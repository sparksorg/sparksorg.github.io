Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

What is this course?

This is a course on Artificial Intelligence which covers the use of the libraries pandas, numpy, and openCV. Building on a fundamental understanding of Python. students will learn how to do image input/output, image functions such as filtering, thresholding, and more using openCV, and will ultimately learn how to do facial detection.

Why take this course?

There are a plethora of benefits to learning Artificial Intelligence. For jobs in data analysis, data science, machine learning, and most computer science fields, knowing artificial intelligence is a necessity. Jobs in the machine learning sector were named the "sexiest jobs of the century", and for good reason. AI is beyond and ahead in every niche, and is used in every corner of the internet, your household technology products, and personal devices.

Understanding how these devices work and everything else that the field of AI can teach you is essential to your future in almost any field. If you stick with the subject and learn more, you will not only have amazing job offers, but also will have endless possibilities for work. Frequently in the news, you see new advancements in this field, whether it be Google's plan to build quantum processors, or local California startups developing new natural language processing or computer vision centered robotic assistants.

On top of this all, AI is an everchanging field. The amazing products you see didn't exist 10 years ago, and its hard to predict how fast AI will grow and where it will go in the near future. So don't wait! Take this introductory course to get a solid foundation for computer vision, and get a headstart into this amazing field.

Algorithms and Data Structures in C++

What is this course?

This is a course which offers a thorough introduction to the massive world of algorithms in C++.

By the end of the course, students will have the ability to code fluently in C++ and utilize a variety of algorithms and data structures, such as but not limited to: Breadth First Search, Depth First Search, Brute Force, Sorting Algorithms, queues, stacks, sets.

Why take this course?

Knowledge of C++ is an essential skill to have in college (Computer Science Major) and in the Computer Science industry. Most of the UC courses, and introductory computer science courses in other colleges, are based in C++.

Even more organizations use C++ extensively including Google and Nvidia. C++ allows you to have a lot of control as to how you use computer resources, and it has immense speed and ability to cheaply use resources.

In addition, students will also learn how to approach seemingly difficult computer science problems and when to use certain algorithms and data structures, skills that are very important for job interviews and industry products.

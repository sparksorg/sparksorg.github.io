iOS Design

What is this course?

The iOS design course will provide students with a wide range of skills ranging from basic syntax to end-to-end application development. Students will gain a thorough understanding of how to use Swift and Xcode and become more experienced in widely used tools such as Firebase and Sketch.

Why take this course?

According to Apple, it has sold 700 million iPhones until today. If we put that on a global perspective, that’s over 9% of the wold population. Creating applications through iOS will allow you to spread your innovative thinking to millions of people around the world. Furthermore, you will gain the necessary skills that many companies will be looking for in a software developer like integrating and using APIs, designing, and object-oriented programming.

One of the greatest benefits of iOS design is that it is one of the easiest to learn as a beginner. It has a storyboard where you can simply drag different components onto your app (e.g. buttons, switches, and text fields) followed by writing the code for the component’s functionality. Another advantage is that it allows you to express your creativity as you can experiment with different backgrounds, color schemes, and components.

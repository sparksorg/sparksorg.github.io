Who are we?

Sparks is a non-profit organization founded by 4 high school juniors at Cupertino High School. With each of us equipped with a wide array of skillsets, we came together with a common goal in mind: help create an impact in our community. With the unfortunate spread of COVID-19, we wanted to find a way to raise money for the nurses, doctors, and countless volunteers who have been putting their lives at risk in order to keep our community safe. To assist them, we developed curriculum for a wide array of programming languages/topics streamlined to help educate the younger generation in understanding the world of technology. Every piece of the curriculum and our live-lectures is free, as we wanted to ensure that all members of the community had access to our work. Instead to raise money, we ask for a donation of any amount you see fit, all of which will be donated to a variety of organizations/hospitals in the Bay Area to help battle the spread of COVID-19. We have a simple mission statement: create a spark of interest for technology in every student's mind.
